The aluminum boat is 5m long and 2m wide. The draft is only 0.05m and the headroom is 1.20m. The hull weight is 420kg and the design category is D.

Water boards and nature managers therefore like to use this 'flat' boat with its blunt nose that makes it easy to get in and out of the boat. Thanks to its aluminum hull, the Alumax Jon 500 is almost indestructible and low-maintenance. The entire boat is welded from 4 mm thick seawater-resistant aluminum. Please note that if a boat goes faster than 20 km per hour there is a boating license requirement.

]]> Bullit 470 outboard https://alumaxboats.nl/en/tenders-en/bullit-470-outboard/ https://alumaxboats.nl/en/tenders-en/bullit-470-outboard/#respond Fri, 18 Dec 2020 07:30:49 +0000 https://alumaxboats.nl/uncategorized/bullit-470-outboard/ The Bullit 470 outboard is an aluminum lightweight boat and offers boating pleasure for friends and small families. The tender boat is 4.70m long and 1.75m wide. The draft is 0.20 and the headroom is 1.15m. The hull weight is 300kg and the design category is C.

The lightweight aluminum tender is characterized by excellent sailing characteristics, stability and efficiently utilized interior space. An advantage of aluminum boats is that they are low maintenance, they can remain in the water in winter and freeze if necessary. Above all, aluminum is foolproof, because it is not broken. This makes our boats resistant to vandalism. All Alumax boats have self-draining valves in the skin so that water easily runs out of the boat. Very handy for cleaning the boat! The Bullit 470 is also available with M.O.B. certification.

Why outboard?

Unlike an inboard engine, the investment in an outboard engine is lower compared to the number of HP. Note: if a boat goes faster than 20 km per hour there is a license requirement. The Bullit 660 outboard offers comfort, convenience and sufficient speed to experience water fun in various forms. The Bullit 660 outboard is also ideal for water sports.

Are you Bullit proof?

The Bullit series consists of 6 indestructible, self-draining and maintenance-free tenders. The aluminum boats are designed and built at our yard in Meppel and promote carefree boating pleasure. Available with inboard diesel engine, outboard gasoline engine or an electric motor. Because aluminum is much lighter than for example a comparable polyester sloop, they can also sail much faster. The Bullit series is designed for these speeds. They remain flat and above all stable sailing, so you do not have to stand. Moreover, they are easy to trailer. Are you going to cast off the ropes?
