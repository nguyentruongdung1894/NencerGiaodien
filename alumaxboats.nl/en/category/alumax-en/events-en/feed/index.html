Our brand new Bullit 600 demo features the DF140BG with improved performance and superior fuel economy. Read on to learn more about Drive-by-Wire and its benefits.

What is Drive-by-Wire?

Previously there was a traditional mechanical connection (with a cable) between the accelerator pedal and the engine. Drive-by-Wire is – in short – an electronic throttle. The engines have a very direct throttle response, they shift silky smooth, the performance is even better and the engines are also more efficient with fuel consumption. The reliability and precise operation of Suzuki’s Drive-by-Wire has been successfully tested for years.

]]> Sometimes you stumble across pictures of previous projects and think… “How proud we can be of this one! What a cool project this was!”

We remember it well: the year the Summer Olympics took place in London, 2012. A special year because for the first time in the long history of OS all countries had at least one woman in their team. We were given the task to buil d17 custom made Bullit 660 inboard boats for the benefit of those special Olympics. The boats would transport VIP guests across the Thames or spend an afternoon on the water.

The Olympics Bullit 660 inboard

The Olympic 660, which we affectionately call it, is essentially the same as a Bullit 660 inboard you usally see sailing on Dutch waters. So what’s so special about this version? The next extras have been added:

  • A spray hood (front);
  • Flexi-teak floors;
  • Wine coolers;
  • Marine radio;
  • Powder coated in- and exterior;
  • Flagpoles;
  • Holders for the fenders;
  • Top lights;
  • Extra lighting on the inside, outside and bottom of the boat;
  • Modifications to the seats: an extra long sun bed.
    in addition, the cushion sets were – and still can be – personalized with own logo or text.

What a beautiful boat!

The versatility of Bullits

The Aluminum Bullit boats are almost all multifunctional. From small to large, different engines, finishes, extras and shapes. As a fanatic water sportsman you can transform your Bullit into the perfect boat that has enough power en resources to let you waterski with ease. But as a fire department officer you can also have fire extinguishing equipment installed. Therefore a Bullit is never the same and different for everyone.
