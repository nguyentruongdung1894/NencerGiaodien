The light aluminium tender is characterised by excellent sailing characteristics, stability and many possibilities due to space. An advantage of aluminium boats is that they are low-maintenance, they can be left in the water during the winter and if necessary they can freeze. Above all, aluminium is vandal-proof, it cannot be broken. This makes our boats resistant to vandalism. The Bullit 720 is multifunctional, that is why this aluminium boat is also used as a work boat with various extras for the desired application. Why outboard?

Unlike an inboard engine, the investment in an outboard engine is lower compared to the number of horsepower. Please note: if a boat goes faster than 20 km per hour, there is a license requirement. The Bullit 720 outboard offers luxury, convenience and sufficient speed to experience water pleasure in various forms. The Bullit 720 outboard is also very suitable for water sports.

Are you Bullit proof?

The Bullit range consists of 6 indestructible, self-bailing and maintenance-free tenders. The aluminium boats are designed and built at our yard in Meppel and promote carefree boating pleasure. Available with inboard diesel engine, outboard petrol engine or an electric motor. Because aluminium is much lighter than for example a comparable polyester sloop, they can also sail much faster.
