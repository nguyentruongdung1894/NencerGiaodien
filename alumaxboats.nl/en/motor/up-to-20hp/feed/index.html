"Who can not sail in The Next, can not sail in anything", said well-known water sports journalist Epco Ongering about the boat. The boat is 5.30 m long and 2.00 m wide. The draft is 0.45m and the headroom is 1.07m. The hull weight is 400kg and the design category is D.

Despite its dimensions, The Next offers a surprising amount of space, making it the ideal boat for a family, rental or group of friends. Thanks to its solid aluminum construction, The Next is hufterproof. The thick bumper ensures that you do not have to worry about a scratch. This comfortable aluminum boat is - like all other Alumax boats - low maintenance and self-draining. The Next is available as an electric sloop or with an outboard motor. All Alumax boats have self-draining devices in the skin, making it easy for water to drain out of the boat. Very handy for cleaning the boat!
